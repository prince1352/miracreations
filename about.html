Meet Munvir Virk

Mira Creations began as a labor of love. In 2022, I took on the challenge of planning and designing the decor for my older sister’s wedding — an experience that awakened a passion I never knew I had. With every draped fabric, floral arrangement, and personalized detail, I discovered a deep appreciation for creating beautiful spaces that bring people joy.

What started as a one-time project quickly blossomed into a growing business. Since then, I’ve poured my heart into building Mira Creations into a trusted name in event decor and jewelry. Today, we help families across Alberta turn their most important celebrations into unforgettable moments — with care, culture, and creativity at the center of everything we do.
