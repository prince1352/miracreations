Meet The Team

Mira Creations is a family-run wedding and event decor business based in Edmonton, Alberta. Founded by a mother and her two daughters, our journey began in 2022 when we took on the challenge of planning a family member's wedding. From selecting jewelry and outfits to designing each floral arrangement and personal detail, we discovered a shared passion for creating unforgettable experiences.

What started as a one-time project quickly evolved into a trusted name in event styling and bridal accessories. We now work with clients across Edmonton to bring their most important celebrations to life — with care, culture, and creativity at the heart of everything we do.
