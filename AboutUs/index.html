Meet The Mira Creations Team

Mira Creations is a family-run wedding and event decor business based in Edmonton, Alberta. Founded by a mother and her two daughters, Mira Creations journey began in 2022 when we took on the challenge of planning a family member's wedding. From selecting jewelry and outfits to designing each floral arrangement and personal detail, we discovered a shared passion for creating unforgettable experiences.

What started as a one-time project quickly evolved into Mira Creations. A trusted name in event decor and bridal accessories. Mira Creations now works with clients across Edmonton to bring their most important celebrations to life, with care, culture, and creativity at the heart of everything we do.
